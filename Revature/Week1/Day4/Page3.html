Human Intervention

What can be done?

As discussed, with our current knowledge it does not seem likely that humans would be able to harness a naturally-occurring wormhole for transportation. Is there any alternative? It is theorized that it may be possible in the distant future, but would require some form of exotic matter or other matter that we do not know of.

Likelihood of wormhole travel

Current models do allow for the existence of wormholes, but use seems highly unlikely any time soon. Maybe one day in the distant future it will become possible, but it will probably not be in any of our lifetimes.


So my Stargate dreams will never come true?

Unfortunately, all the evidence seems to point towards that being the case. Sorry!
